Social Responsibility

I Jyoshnamayee Parida, I am committed to making a positive impact on society. Here are some of the key areas where I focus on social responsibility:

Education and Literacy

I strongly believe in providing quality education to all children, regardless of their background. Through various initiatives, we aim to improve access to education and promote literacy in our communities.

Environment Conservation

Preserving our environment for future generations is of utmost importance to me. We undertake various initiatives to promote sustainable practices, reduce pollution, and protect our natural resources.

Healthcare and Well-being

Access to affordable healthcare and promoting well-being are essential for a thriving society. We work towards ensuring quality healthcare services and raising awareness about healthy living.

Community Development

Building strong and inclusive communities is a priority. We support initiatives that empower individuals, promote social cohesion, and enhance the overall development of our communities.

Gender Equality

Promoting gender equality is crucial for a just and fair society. We strive to eliminate gender-based discrimination, empower women, and create equal opportunities for all.

Youth Empowerment

Investing in the potential of young people is key to shaping a better future. We support programs that provide skills training, mentorship, and opportunities for youth to thrive.
