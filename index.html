Jyoshnamayee Parida

"State Sr. Vice President of BYJD at Biju Janata Dal Odisha"

Welcome to the official website of Jyoshnamayee Parida. We strive to make a positive impact on our community and bring about meaningful change. Here, you will find information about our goals, values, and achievements. Our mission is to serve the people and create a better future for all. A person with positive posture, self-esteemed and an expeditious learner, I love to interact with people of diverse communities and understand them because I feel that, the more you interact with people the more you get acquainted with each other and the better you understand each other and it creates a special bond between all of us. I have always Idolize my role model late. Shri. Biju Patnaik and endeavour to follow his footstep to achieve prosperity in every field. I always venerate and learn from Mr. Naveen Patnaik because he follows the footsteps of his father and possesses all the qualities of his father. Whenever I feel that I am stuck in any situation then I look up to my idols to find a way out because I feel that the best way out of difficulty is through it.

I was born and brought up in Kendrapada and I have proximately visually perceived the lives of people in Kendrapada. From my childhood I always wanted to help people and whenever I got the chance to interact with them I never fail to do so. I understand the people and their needs because interacting with people is something I am proficient at.
